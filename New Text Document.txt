When NGOs plan activities like tree planting or food donation drives, organizers usually ask for opinions in WhatsApp groups and end up scrolling through hundreds of replies trying to figure out what everyone wants. It's messy and time-consuming. I chose this problem because it's real and happens all the time in small NGOs that can't afford fancy tools. A simple polling app fixes this directly the organizer creates a poll, shares the link, volunteers vote in one tap, and the results show up instantly as a clean chart. Small tool, but it genuinely makes coordination faster and easier.
